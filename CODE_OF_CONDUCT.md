# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we, as members and moderators of this community, pledge to ensure a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, sexual orientation, race, or religion.

## Standards

### Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language  
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences  
- Accepting constructive criticism graciously  
- Focusing on the community's best interests  
- Showing empathy towards others  

### Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Using sexualized language, images, or inappropriate remarks  
- Trolling, insulting comments, personal or political attacks  
- Publishing private information without explicit consent  
- Harassment in public or private settings  

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies across all community spaces (e.g., forums, online or offline events) and when representing the community publicly.

## Enforcement
If you observe a violation of the Code of Conduct, please report it by sending an email to **[community@synergycodes.com](mailto:community@synergycodes.com)**.  
Each report will be thoroughly reviewed and investigated, and appropriate actions will be taken based on the situation.  
Specific enforcement policies may be outlined separately.

Moderators reserve the right to remove any comments or contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.  
Additionally, members engaging in inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful behavior may face temporary or permanent bans from the community.
